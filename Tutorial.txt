1.First download the RoboTIX.css file
2.Go into the chrome store and type in the search bar "Stylus"
3.Install the extension
4.Go to the Roblox website and press on the Stylus button
5.Click Create Style
6.Now press on the "Import" button and drag and drop the RoboTIX.css file
7.Press overwrite Style
8.Now you can give it a name and then press on the "Save" button
9.Go to the Roblox site and press on the Stylus button again
10.Select the theme with the name you gave it

I really hope that the tutorial was clearly enough! :)
